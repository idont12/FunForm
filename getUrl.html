Google Form URL Builder

Create and edit custom URLs for your Google Form UI website.

Instructions

▶ How to get Form ID
Open Google Form preview.

Example:
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLXXXXX/viewform

Copy the text between:
/e/ and /viewform
▶ How to get Entry IDs
• Open Google Form editor
• Click the 3 dots
• Press "Get pre-filled link"
• Fill fake answers
• Press "Get Link"
• Copy the URL

Example:
entry.486951529
▶ Dropdown Structure
Dropdown questions MUST have exactly 4 parts:

Entry ID
Type
Question Title
Options

Example:

&q=entry.486951529|dropdown|What games do u usually play?|Platformer,Shooter,Puzzle,RPG
